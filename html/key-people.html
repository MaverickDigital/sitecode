CO-FOUNDERS

Belal “Bella” El-Harazin – Founder and CEO

“Everything begins and ends with culture. Do what is right for the customer and the rest will take care of itself.” Belal’s career has spanned over 20 years in transformational technology companies, last serving as Industry Vice President for Adobe Systems. At Adobe, he won many awards for helping usher some of the largest and groundbreaking digital transformations with some of the world’s most recognized B2C and B2B brands. Prior to Adobe, Belal worked with Riverside Capital helping turn around and grow Brandmuscle and WhatCounts, two of Riverside Capital’s successful digital marketing exits. In his free time, Belal spends his time with his wife Khadija and son Ryan. He also tries to get in as much golf and Muay Thai in his schedule as possible.  
 

Rob Gonda – Founder and Chief Digital Officer

Rob is a successful digital entrepreneur & digital transformation executive. A Senior Executive with proven successes at leading innovation, technology, and marketing in multiple environments. Rob has led growth and transformation for multiple companies ranging from iconic global brands like McDonald’s to startups. At McDonald’s, Rob spearheaded a transformation centered around modernization of the customer experience and becoming a data & insights driven-business, resulting in the business more than doubling market cap during his leadership. He is a dynamic thought leader, combining unique approaches to technology, business, digital, and marketing strategies. He is results-oriented and highly effective at adapting to rapidly changing environments and resolving mission critical issues to ensure bottom line success. Focused on trends, strategies, innovation, competitive landscape, product, and complex developmental methodologies, standards, and future technology direction.
