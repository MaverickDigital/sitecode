KEY PEOPLE

CO-FOUNDERS

Belal “Bella” El-Harazin – Founder and CEO

“Everything begins and ends with culture. Do what is right for the customer and the rest will take care of itself.” Belal’s career has spanned over 20 years in transformational technology companies, last serving as Industry Vice President for Adobe Systems. At Adobe, he won many awards for helping usher some of the largest and groundbreaking digital transformations with some of the world’s most recognized B2C and B2B brands. Prior to Adobe, Belal worked with Riverside Capital helping turn around and grow Brandmuscle and WhatCounts, two of Riverside Capital’s successful digital marketing exits. In his free time, Belal spends his time with his wife Khadija and son Ryan. He also tries to get in as much golf and Muay Thai in his schedule as possible.  
 

Rob Gonda – Founder and Chief Digital Officer

Rob is a successful digital entrepreneur & digital transformation executive. He was recruited to lead the digital transformation of one of the most iconic brands in history, McDonalds. He was directly responsible for a corporate budget of $80 million a year, leading strategy and execution of digital technology ranging from Customer Facing products, technology platforms, and data and analytics. Most visible examples are web, kiosk, and mobile channels, delivering e-commerce, marketing, loyalty, and CRM capabilities, and all reporting and business intelligence to drive it.
